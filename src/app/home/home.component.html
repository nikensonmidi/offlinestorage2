Firebase PoC (proof of concept)

For this PoC, we will be able to modify the information and be able to make an update.

Just head over to Dispatchlog .

Then switch to offline mode on your phone. You should still be able to click on the link above and still have data available.

In addition, you should be able to add, edit and delete a record while you are offline.

All offline changes will be stored locally. Once you switch back to online mode, the app will push your changes to remote storage.
